# Nova Content Moderation - Custom Policies
# ==========================================
# Maximum: 10,000 characters
#
# Format: #policy [number]: [description]
# Each policy should be on its own line.
#
# Examples:
#policy 1: block all spam and advertising content of all kind for example
#policy 2: warn on mild profanity, block on severe profanity
#policy 3: ban 7 days for hate speech or slurs
#policy 4: escalate threats of violence for human review
#policy 5: block sharing of personal information (doxxing)
